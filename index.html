Plumber

I'm Mario, "The most famous plumber in the Mushroom Kingdom". My life is a constant adventure. One day I'm fixing pipes in my house and the next I'm jumping over Goombas and Koopas to rescue Princess Peach from the clutches of the evil Bowser.

S k i l l s

I've got a whole bag of tricks up my sleeve! One of my most iconic abilities is jumping. I can leap over obstacles, enemies, and even huge gaps. But that's not all I can also run super fast, and with a power-up, I can even fly.

I'm pretty handy with my hands, too. I can ground pound to defeat enemies and break blocks, and with a fire flower, I can throw fireballs with pinpoint accuracy. And let's not forget my ability to swim and dive underwater.

Over the years, I've picked up some other awesome skills. I can shrink down to fit into tiny spaces, grow giant to smash everything in my path, and even turn into a raccoon to glide through the air. No matter what the challenge, I always find a way to overcome it!"

Jump and Run

My legs are like springs! I can make incredible leaps to reach high platforms, dodge dangerous obstacles, and of course, squash those pesky Goombas. With every jump, I feel the adrenaline pumping through my veins!

Fire

My fingers transform into cannons that shoot fireballs at top speed! I can burn Piranha Plants, destroy brick blocks, and even defeat the toughest Koopas. It's amazing the power I feel when I throw a fireball and watch it hit its target!

Swim

I'm like a fish in the water! I can dive deep to find hidden treasures and swim at top speed to escape the dangers of the deep sea. With a good pair of flippers, no ocean can stop me!"

My experience

They call me the king of platforming! But let's be honest, I've probably broken more blocks than any other being in the universe. And don't even get me started on my coin collection.
